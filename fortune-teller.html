Fortune teller

A paper oracle, folded and unfolded

Design · March 2026

pick a number

The paper fortune teller, a schoolyard oracle made from a single folded sheet. Pick a number, watch it open and close, pick again, and peek beneath the flap. The future was always in your hands.

This is a recreation built with SVG and vanilla JavaScript. Each fold and reveal is animated to capture the tactile rhythm of the original paper game.
